Frequently Asked Questions

I am new to fasting. Which schedule should I start with?

Do not over analyze. Just start. Aim for 8 hours while you sleep. Feel like you have more in you? Start aiming for 12 hours. Work up to 16, 23, or even 40.

Will this help me lose weight?

Intermittent fasting can support weight loss by helping you eat fewer calories. Fasting Hours helps you stay consistent with your fasting schedule.

What if I get really hungry during my fast?

Until you've taught your brain otherwise, it will want to eat. Stay hydrated. Tracking helps you take a moment to remember who is the boss of you.

Is it safe to track everything locally?

If you clear your browser's data, you will lose you fasting history. You can export and import your data at any time. Want cloud backup and sync? Get lifetime access.

Can I use this if I have health conditions?

Always consult your doctor before starting any fasting regimen, especially with health conditions. Fasting Hours is a tracking tool, not medical advice.

What if I break my fast early?

No problem! Log when you stopped and start fresh next time. Consistency matters more than perfection. Use the data to understand what works for you.
